---
title: Unwritten
date: 2025-01-27
---

<div class = "stanza">
A Photograph - colours fade<br>
A quiet canvas left betrayed<br>
No face to anchor, no story to frame,<br>
Just shadows whispering an absent name<br>
The void persists, yet beauty reflects,<br>
a Photograph without a Subject<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Room - chairs line the floor<br>
Movement only outside the door<br>
No person, not a single child<br>
Just flickering lights, going wild<br>
The void persists, silence repairs<br>
A Room filled with empty chairs<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Letter - words from the heart<br>
Emotions, no less than art<br>
No recipient, no sender sought<br>
Just words adrift, a fleeting thought<br>
The void persists, a connection is drawn<br>
A Letter to No One<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Symphony - in silence loud<br>
The wind, still yet unbound<br>
No notes, not a tone is heard<br>
Just melodies of nothing, no chirping birds<br>
The void persists, where absence gloats<br>
A Symphony without Notes<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
An Address - leading to nought<br>
A search for places never sought<br>
No roads to tread, no rivers to follow<br>
Just empty winds, a whisper hollow<br>
The void persists, where none may roam<br>
An Address without a Home<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Bookmark - a fragile slip<br>
Memories held through thick and thin<br>
No place to point, no tale to hold<br>
Just hopes for dreams yet untold<br>
The void persists, defying age<br>
A Bookmark without a Page<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Title - bold and profound<br>
It's promise vast, yet unbound<br>
No tale, no words to unfold<br>
Just potential, silent yet bold<br>
The void persists, undefined glory<br>
A Title without a Story<br>
</div>
