---
title: Unwritten
date: 2025-01-27
---

<div class = "stanza">
A Photograph - colours fade
A quiet canvas left betrayed
No face to anchor, no story to frame,
Just shadows whispering an absent name
The void persists, yet beauty reflects,
a Photograph without a Subject
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Room - chairs line the floor
Movement only outside the door
No person, not a single child
Just flickering lights, going wild
The void persists, silence repairs
A Room filled with empty chairs
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Letter - words from the heart
Emotions, no less than art
No recipient, no sender sought
Just words adrift, a fleeting thought
The void persists, a connection is drawn
A Letter to No One
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Symphony - in silence loud
The wind, still yet unbound
No notes, not a tone is heard
Just melodies of nothing, no chirping birds
The void persists, where absence gloats
A Symphony without Notes
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
An Address - leading to nought
A search for places never sought
No roads to tread, no rivers to follow
Just empty winds, a whisper hollow
The void persists, where none may roam
An Address without a Home
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Bookmark - a fragile slip
Memories held through thick and thin
No place to point, no tale to hold
Just hopes for dreams yet untold
The void persists, defying age
A Bookmark without a Page
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
A Title - bold and profound
It's promise vast, yet unbound
No tale, no words to unfold
Just potential, silent yet bold
The void persists, undefined glory
A Title without a Story
</div>
