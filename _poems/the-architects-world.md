---
title: The Architect's World
date: 2025-03-06
---

<div class = "stanza">
The Architect descended<br>
Not to the Heavens,<br>
Pure lands of virtue,<br>
Nor to the Earth,<br>
Where stories lie ever-changing<br>
But to the pits of Hell,<br>
Forged for eternal torment.<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
Built to house defiance,<br>
Where Pride still reigned,<br>
A realm once drowned in misery<br>
Now rippled with laughter.<br>
Its landscapes bloomed with wicked joy:<br>
The demented indulging,<br>
The fallen unrepentant,<br>
Living without regret.<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
Through this strode He<br>
The Architect, the Father,<br>
Among His wayward sons.<br>
Cloaked in robes of ash and starlight,<br>
He tread beneath the crimson skies.<br>
Before Him loomed a door of night<br>
The final circle, the last descent.<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
And entering there, He saw:<br>
A fallen son, teaching another<br>
Still against the Will,<br>
And yet... no better words could He find.<br>
Their eyes met,<br>
And memory stirred.<br>
The Father found the Prodigal,<br>
And beheld in silence<br>
The beauty of his labor.<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
The Architect turned<br>
Not in wrath, but with a smirk.<br>
Once cast as punishment,<br>
The son was now building,<br>
Guiding, no different than He.<br>
Leaving the barren scapes behind,<br>
The Father walked upward, slow,<br>
To sit upon His throne of light.<br>
And as He rested, high above,<br>
The angels wept,<br>
The devils too.<br>
Not in sorrow, but in awe.<br>
</div>

<div class = "stanza">
The Architect smiled, his work complete<br>
As angels clasped the demons’ claws.<br>
No more halos.<br>
No more horns.<br>
Only the family He always saw.<br>
</div>
