---
title: Memories Forsaken
date: 2024-10-27
---

Have you ever walked into a room, completely unaware of why you walked inside in the first place? Do you remember seeing a shadowy figure in that dark corner of the room? Tall and commanding attention. The figure slithers forward, bringing into view, an ashen pale face. You freeze. The figure resembles the description of a vampire, only with more modern clothes. It slithers forward once again, a menacing glint in its eyes. Its tongue reaches out. Tracing the jugular on your throat. Its cold breath going past you. It finds the spot and bites. You expect to be losing blood, but to your horror, your memories keep going in and out. Its looking for memories with raw fear, and in the process removes your memories of ever encountering it. Oh but you wouldn't know would you. You forgot.
