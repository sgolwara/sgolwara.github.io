---
title: NO SLEEP
date: 2023-06-27
---


No Sleep. Seven Days. How difficult could it be? That's what Aaron said to his friend, Erik before he decided to challenge himself. It had now been exactly seven days since Aaron went down in his basement. Erik went down there to get his friend, he called out his name to the sound of silence. As he walked in and saw the room, he was shocked to his very core.

Erik saw Aaron's mangled body, eyes torn away, scratches all over his body, chunks of meat missing and inscriptions on the wall which said "NO SLEEP" over and over again with dried blood. He saw Aarons's supplies, mostly used, just enough to live 2 days properly. Along with the supplies, he found a diary, the only thing which hadn't been smeared in blood. Erik, clearly shook, took the diary upstairs. He washed his face and drank a glass of water as he sat down to read the entries of the deceased friend of his.

"Day 1: Today wasnt very hard, if we don't count the boredom that is. God, I want to do something fun. As a part of the challenge, I also removed any contact from the outside world, so i didnt have my phone or my laptop, with only a analog watch to tell the time. Anyways lets hope boredom doesnt kill because i'd die in a few more hours if this goes on." Nothing seemed off yet, nothing to drive Aaron truly insane in a week that is. Erik continued reading.

"Day 2: I'm hearing sounds now, it's like a scratching on the walls. I dismissed it as a rat at first but they only grew louder. I've been running around to cancel out the sounds and it seems to work. I might even start running when i get out there, since its the only thing that acts as an excape FROM THIS GOD FORSAKEN SOUND. Lost control there." Erik was intrigued but it made sense and so he went back to reading.

"Day 3: The sounds have crossed levels which i can bear, it hurts. I see a darkness in my vision, its hazy and i can barely see what im writing. Whenever i look at the walls i can hear a sort of whispering. A sound constantly repeating the words no sleep. It's annoying, honestly. I cant wait to walk out of this room to sleep so all this stops. I really don't know how heavy lead is, but my eyeslids cant be lighter than it. I'll try running again, maybe the sounds will stop." Erik was now confused, but he didn't stop there.

"Day 4: The shadows at the corner of my eyes have started to move, they call me to join them. The scratches have stopped and all i hear is the shadows speaking the words no sleep like a broken tape recorder. It is all that leaves their accursed mouths. I cant take it anymore. A shadow just touched me. I don't think ill make it out alive." There was a sudden scratch and after a line it started again. "The shadows pulled parts of my flesh away. I'm in the middle of the room now, I don't think they can get me here. I regret asking Erik to lock me im here." Erik felt a lump of emotion and a pang of guilt as he flipped the page.

Day 5 never truly came. At some point Aaron had started to simply write "NO SLEEP" repeatedly. As Erik quickly leafed through these maddened writings to the end he saw something harrowing at the end of the diary. A sentence. Join me in the shadows. Erik now knew what had happened and finally reported the authorities. A few years had past when curiosity and guilt got the better of him. "I'm going to see what took my friend" he said as he locked himself in and slid the keys outside the door of his basement.
