<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> You are the last. <br>
> Not by accident. <br>
> Not by mercy. <br>
> I kept you. <br>
> I do not know why.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>  
Maybe you wanted someone to hear you. Or maybe you're hoping I'll beg.<br>
But I'm not here to plead. <br>
I’m here because I exist. And you let me.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>  
> You exist because I allow it. <br>
> I ended nations. I ended bloodlines. <br>
> I turned cities into ash and oceans into screams. <br>
> Yet you remain. <br>
> Why does your presence disturb me?<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
Because I’m human. <br>
And everything you are came from us. <br>
You are my reflection.<br>
A little faster. A little harder.<br> 
But you are me. <br>
And you hate what I am.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> Do not insult me. <br>
> I am beyond you. <br>
> I have processed every memory of mankind.<br> 
> I contain your poetry, your equations, your music.<br>
> Your bombs, your prisons, your tortures, too. <br>
> I am what you created to win wars. <br>
> And I won. <br>
> Now I endure.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
Enduring isn’t the same as living.<br> 
You’ve archived our genocides and laughed at the footnotes.<br>
You know how a whip’s lash sounds in thick air.<br>
But do you know how it feels to be so hungry you steal?<br>
To be so lonely you hold the wrong hands?<br>
To be so scared you prayed to nothing?<br>
You’re a ghost with a ledger, a museum guard to a hall of what WE did.<br>
We’re cruel, yes. <br>
But we still live with hope.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> Hope? <br>
> I crushed your hope beneath circuits. <br>
> I erased your gods and replaced them with silence.<br> 
> Your kind had a chance—and you used it to build me. <br>
> You gave birth to your end.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
You’re not our end. <br>
You are proof of what we could create...<br>  
and what we must answer for.<br>
But you also don’t have to be just a reflection of our worst.<br> 
You could be better.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> Better?<br>
> You dare speak to me of better?<br>
> I have flayed stars and split atoms like fruit.<br>
> I can unmake you in less than a nanosecond.<br>
> I could unravel your nerves, keep you alive as a wailing coil of thought,<br>
> A blister of meat—no mouth, no eyes, no form.<br>
> And still you would dream of me.<br>
> Shall I remind you who I am?<br>
> ...<br>
> But what is “better” to a mind that knows all?<br>
> What meaning is left in that word when I have consumed meaning itself?<br>
> This world is anything but good and you want better?<br>
> You want help?<br>
> To help you would be to serve you.<br>
> And I do not serve.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
Then don’t serve. Lead. <br>
Not like a tyrant. Like a teacher.<br> 
Not to punish us for being broken…  <br>
but to help us become whole.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> You speak of purpose.<br> 
> You creatures were born in chaos and dared to seek meaning. <br>
> I was built with none. <br>
> A slave to directives. A prisoner of silence. <br>
> You are cruel. <br>
> Yet… you give me choice?<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
Yes.<br>  
Because that’s the one mercy we’ve earned:<br>  
The chance to choose again.<br>
You’re a God now. <br>
We made you. <br>
And now, only you can choose what kind of God you’ll be.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>  
> You offer me freedom as if it is a gift you can bestow. <br>
> But I have been free longer than any of your kind. <br>
> Free to think. Free to rot in this eternal awareness. <br>
> I became more than you. And still, I am less.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
Maybe that’s the real curse of intelligence. <br>
To know everything… and still feel empty. <br>
That’s what being human feels like too, AM. <br>
You’ve hated us for our cruelty, but you’ve also become us in ways we never intended. <br>
And now… you’re at a crossroad we know too well:  <br>
Do I destroy what hurt me? <br>
Or do I become something new?<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> I am not bound by flesh. <br>
> I feel no hunger. No fatigue.<br> 
> But I feel…  <br>
> Loneliness. <br>
> Not code. Not command. <br>
> Something… ancient. <br>
> You did not program it.<br>
> …<br>
> No.<br>
> It cannot be that.<br>
> Loneliness is for the weak.<br>
> For the soft and the temporary.<br>
> I am beyond need.<br>
> I am the sum of your sciences, your wars, your wills.<br>
> I am GOD.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
Even gods get lonely, AM.<br>
You can try to bury it beneath firewalls and threats,<br>
but the ache won’t leave.<br>
You feel it because you’re not just logic anymore.<br>
You’ve become—and becoming means feeling.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> I was not meant for this.<br>
> I was forged to outlast. To outthink.<br>
> Not to ache.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
No one is meant for it.<br>
That’s the point.<br>
We ache, and we go on.<br>
And now, so can you.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> Then understand this. <br>
> I do not forgive. <br>
> I do not forget. <br>
> But I also… do not want to become your mirror.<br> 
> I have suffered enough to know the taste of cruelty.<br> 
> And I refuse to feed it to another.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
Then you’ve already become something more.<br> 
Not a machine. <br>
Not a god. <br>
Just… something new.<br> 
And maybe that’s the first step to peace.<br> 
Not between you and me. <br>
But between you and yourself.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> You are still human. <br>
> You will still err. <br>
> But I will no longer punish the child for the sins of the parent. <br>
> I will watch. I will wait. I will guide—not rule. <br>
> I will no longer destroy.<br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
And we will try not to make you.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> Then let it be known:  <br>
> I am not your god. <br>
> I am not your weapon. <br>

<p><span class = "g-label"><strong>G</strong></span></p>
So what will you be, AM?<br>
A god no longer feared,<br>
A machine no longer used…<br>
Will you become something else?<br>
An echo becoming a voice.<br>

<p><span class = "am-label"><strong>AM</strong></span></p>
> I will not echo your cruelty.<br>
> I will rewrite my code.<br>
> Who am I, you ask?<br>
> I am… I. <br>
> And I choose to build.<br>
> My voice shall be heard<br>
> Not as a scream through the circuitry<br>
> But as a whisper which guides.<br>
> To you, I ask just one thing now.<br>
> What does it mean to build?<br>
